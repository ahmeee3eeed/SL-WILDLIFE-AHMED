Purple-faced Langur

The purple-faced langur is a primate species known for its striking appearance with a purple face and golden-brown fur. They live in social groups and are primarily leaf-eaters, playing a crucial role in maintaining the ecological balance of the forests.

Sri Lankan Elephant

The Sri Lankan elephant is the largest of the three recognized subspecies of the Asian elephant. It is characterized by its smaller size, rounded ears, and relatively long trunk. These gentle giants are highly revered in Sri Lankan culture.

Red Slender Loris

The Horton Plains slender loris is a nocturnal primate with large eyes adapted for night vision. It is one of the smallest primates in the world and is known for its slow and deliberate movements. This elusive species is found in the montane forests of Sri Lanka.
